ES5 introduced the bind() method to set the value of a function's this regardless of how it's called, and ES2015 introduced arrow functions which don't provide their own this binding (it retains the this value of the enclosing lexical context). (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Operators/this)
